Kelud umbrella cloud source comparison with satellite observations
of the spreading cloud.

Wind files needed: NCEP reanalysis, 2014
Assuming you have volcano-ash3d-metreader installed in the default location, run:
 /opt/USGS/bin/autorun_scripts/get_NCEP_50YearReanalysis.sh 2014

Ref: Mastin and Van Eaton, Comparing simulation of umbrella-cloud growth and ash
transport with observations from Pinatubo, Kelud, and Calbuco Volcanoes,
Atmosphere, v11,1038, 2020, doi:10.3390/atmos11101038
