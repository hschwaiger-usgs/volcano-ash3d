Kasatochi cloud run, comparing with satellite ash retrievals (in the style of
Wen-Rose), using a source term calculated from an inversion on one of the
satellite images.

Wind files needed: NCEP reanalysis, 2008
Assuming you have volcano-ash3d-metreader installed in the default location, run:
 /opt/USGS/bin/autorun_scripts/get_NCEP_50YearReanalysis.sh 2008
