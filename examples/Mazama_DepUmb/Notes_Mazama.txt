Mazama umbrella cloud comparison with deposits.

Wind files needed: NCEP reanalysis, 2010
Assuming you have volcano-ash3d-metreader installed in the default location, run:
 /opt/USGS/bin/autorun_scripts/get_NCEP_50YearReanalysis.sh 2010

Ref: Buckland, et al, Modelling the transport and deposition of ash following a
magnitude 7 eruption: the distal Mazama tephra, Bulletin of Volcanology, v84,87,
2022,doi:10.1007/s00445-022-01593-1
